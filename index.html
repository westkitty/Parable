Controls

  • Move the pointer or drag a finger across the island to guide the divine hand.
  • Hold and draw a clockwise spiral on the land to arm a ritual.
  • Draw a second glyph before the ritual expires to cast a miracle.
  • Hold Shift while dragging, or use right mouse drag, to pan the camera.
  • Use the mouse wheel or trackpad scroll to zoom the world view.
  • Hotkeys 1 through 0 now highlight gesture vocabulary for learning only.
  • Use Restart World to rebuild the simulation cleanly.
